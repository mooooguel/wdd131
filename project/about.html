About Me

My name is Michael Watson and my creator ID is mooooguel. I am pursuing a career in Game Design, particularly in level design and world building. Currently, I am studying at Brigham Young University-Idaho and getting a degree in Software Development. I recently got engaged to my fiance Sydney who is the inspiration of one of my current projects, 'The Girl Who Loves Chipotle'.
